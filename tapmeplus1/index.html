How it works
- Tap any square: it goes up by exactly 1.
- If that creates a connected group of 3 or more equal numbers, they fuse into one next-level tile.
- Then the board collapses downward in a jewel / Tetris-style drop.
- A successful fuse gives you +1 move back and adds score.
- No clear? You spend 1 move.
- When moves hit 0, game over. Tiny tragedy.
